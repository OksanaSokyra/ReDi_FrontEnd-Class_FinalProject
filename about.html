Who we are:

We are sure that every living hero deserves to know, that his or her work are appreciated.

That's why we are colecting the stories about real heroes acrose the world. If you have a story about a person (persons) who have changed or saved your life, please feel free to contact us. We always try to find the heroes personaly and share with them your appreciation.
